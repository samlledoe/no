Noli Me Tangere: Characters
FRIAR DAMASO VERDOLAGAS Father Damaso is a former parish priest in San Diego who antagonizes Ibarra. He does not understand the filipino language despite hearing filipino confessions for about 20 years.
JUAN
CRISOSTOMO IBARRA
The son of Don Rafael Ibarra. This character is Rizal's point of view of himself.
FRIAR HERNANDO
SIBYLA
A dominican friar and a former teacher of Crisostomo Ibarra.
KAPITAN TIAGO
Kapitan Tiago is the symbol of rich filipino men who oppress their own countrymen in Jose Rizal's perspective.
DONYA VICTORINA
Donya Victorina is a woman who pretends to be a Meztiza while attempting to speak the Spanish language.
